Mission & Goals

An open, community-run cybersecurity group for security practitioners, students, tinkerers, and curious minds in Northern Snohomish County. We meet, build, learn, and collaborate — on our own terms.

Open by defaultAll group projects are open source. We build in the open.
No gatekeepingWork on what interests you. No group veto over individual research.
Members own their workWhat you bring to SSH remains yours. Full stop.
Low frictionNo dues, no bureaucracy, no mandatory participation.
