Mission

An open, community-run cybersecurity group for security practitioners, students, tinkerers, and curious minds in the Pacific Northwest. We meet, build, learn, and collaborate — on our own terms.

Core Principles
Open by default All group projects are open source. We build in the open.
No gatekeeping Work on what interests you. No group veto over individual research.
Members own their work What you bring to SSH remains yours. Full stop.
Low friction No dues, no bureaucracy, no mandatory participation. Show up when it's useful.
What We Do
  • Monthly meetups — Snohomish County Library community spaces
  • CTF participation and skills workshops
  • Member research presentations and project demos
  • Collaborative open source security tooling
  • Networking with the broader PNW security community
Join

There are no membership requirements. Anyone interested in security, privacy, hacking, or adjacent technical fields is welcome.

Continued participation is membership.
